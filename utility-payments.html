Common Questions Answered

Which utility providers are supported?

We support all major utility providers across India, including state electricity boards, water supply boards, gas companies, and telecom operators.

How much commission do I earn per bill?

Commission rates vary by service type and transaction amount. Contact our support team for detailed commission structure.

What if a transaction fails?

Failed transactions are automatically refunded within 24 hours. Our support team is available 24/7 to assist with any issues.

Do I need BBPS certification?

No, as a Cashbez agent, you don't need separate BBPS certification. We handle all regulatory compliances.

Can I pay my own bills through Cashbez?

Yes, you can use Cashbez to pay your own utility bills and earn commission on your transactions.
