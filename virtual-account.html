Frequently Asked Questions

What is a virtual account?

A virtual account is a digital account that allows you to receive payments without needing a physical bank account. Each virtual account has its own unique account number and can be used for receiving payments from customers.

How secure are virtual accounts?

Our virtual accounts are protected by bank-grade security measures, including end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication, and real-time fraud monitoring. We comply with all major security standards and regulations.

Can I use virtual accounts internationally?

Yes, our virtual accounts support multiple currencies and can be used for international transactions. We support payments from over 150 countries worldwide.
