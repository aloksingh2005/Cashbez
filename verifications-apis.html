Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is your verification service?

Our verification service uses bank-grade encryption and follows industry best practices for security. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we're compliant with major security standards including GDPR and SOC 2.

What types of documents do you support?

We support a wide range of identity documents including passports, national IDs, driver's licenses, and more from over 150 countries. Our system is regularly updated to support new document types.

How long does verification take?

Most verifications are completed in real-time, typically within 2-3 seconds. In some cases, additional manual review may be required, which can take up to 24 hours.
