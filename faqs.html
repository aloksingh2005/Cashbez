General Questions

What is Cashbez?

Cashbez is a leading digital payment platform that enables secure and convenient transactions for individuals and businesses across India. We offer a wide range of payment solutions including UPI, wallet, and card payments.

How do I get started with Cashbez?

Getting started is easy! Simply download our app from the App Store or Google Play Store, sign up with your mobile number, and complete the verification process. You'll be ready to make payments in minutes.

Is Cashbez safe to use?

Yes, Cashbez employs state-of-the-art security measures including end-to-end encryption, two-factor authentication, and regular security audits to ensure your transactions and data remain safe and secure.

Account & Security

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen. You'll receive an OTP on your registered mobile number. Enter the OTP and follow the instructions to set a new password.

How can I update my profile information?

You can update your profile information by going to the "Profile" section in the app. Click on "Edit Profile" to modify your details. Some changes may require additional verification.

What should I do if I notice suspicious activity?

If you notice any suspicious activity, immediately contact our 24/7 customer support team. We recommend changing your password and enabling two-factor authentication for additional security.

Payments & Transactions

What payment methods are accepted?

Cashbez accepts various payment methods including UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and digital wallets. We also support international payments through our global payment network.

How do I track my transactions?

You can track all your transactions in the "Transactions" section of the app. Each transaction includes detailed information such as date, time, amount, and status.

What are the transaction limits?

Transaction limits vary based on your account type and verification status. Basic accounts have lower limits, while fully verified accounts enjoy higher transaction limits. Check the "Account Limits" section for details.

Business Solutions

How do business transaction fees work?
